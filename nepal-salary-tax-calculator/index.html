🟡 What is the 1% tax?
The 1% flat tax on the first slab (Rs. 500,000 or Rs. 600,000) is applied only if you do not contribute to SSF or a Pension Scheme.

✅ If you contribute to SSF:
Your first Rs. 500,000 (or Rs. 600,000 if married) is completely tax-free.

💡 Example

If you are unmarried with Rs. 700,000 taxable income:

  • With SSF: First 500,000 → 0%, Next 200,000 → 10% = Rs. 20,000
  • Without SSF: First 500,000 → 1% = Rs. 5,000, Next 200,000 → 10% = Rs. 20,000
